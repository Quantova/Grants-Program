# Frequently Asked Questions

## Who can apply?

Anyone — individuals, teams, and companies. The focus is on strong technical work
that adds value to the Quantova ecosystem.

## How do I apply?

Either submit at **https://quantova.org/grants/**, or open a pull request to this
repository using the [application template](../applications/application-template.md).
Questions: **Grants@quantova.org**.

## What does it cost / how much can I get?

Nothing to apply. Grant size depends on the level and scope (see
[process.md](process.md)). Grants are paid in **QTOV**.

## In what currency are grants paid?

In **QTOV**, the native asset of Quantova, after delivery and approval of each
milestone, to the payment address in your application.

## What kinds of projects do you fund?

See [categories.md](categories.md). Broadly: developer tooling, infrastructure, QVM
and smart contracts, post-quantum cryptography, bridges, and research/education.

## What do you *not* fund?

Projects with no clear relation to Quantova, token launches or speculative/financial
products, closed-source work, marketing-only efforts, and re-packaging existing
software without meaningful new contribution.

## Does my project have to be open-source?

Yes. Grant deliverables are open-source (Apache 2.0 / MIT / Unlicense or similar).

## How long should my roadmap be?

We recommend a scope that fits within about three months, structured as roughly
1 milestone ≈ 1 month, with testable deliverables.

## What happens after I submit?

The grants committee reviews your application, may ask questions or request changes,
and decides whether to accept it. If accepted, you agree terms, then build and
deliver milestone by milestone (see [milestone-delivery.md](milestone-delivery.md)).

## Can I change my application after submitting?

Yes, but changes require re-evaluation by the committee. For significant changes
during development, open a new PR modifying your application or email
Grants@quantova.org.

## I have an idea but can't build it myself.

Suggest it as an RFP — see [../rfps/README.md](../rfps/README.md). The grants team
may turn it into a Request for Proposals for a capable team to pick up.

## Is there a referral bonus?

If a referral program is active, the details and any bonus will be described on
**https://quantova.org/grants/**. Include your referrer in the application's
referral section.

## How do I contact the team?

Email **Grants@quantova.org** or visit **https://quantova.org/grants/**.
